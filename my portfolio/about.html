About Victor Gabriel

Victor Okwegada is a tech evangelist, community leader, and product designer based in Nigeria. He is passionate about the power of technology to change the world for the better, and he is committed to spreading this message far and wide. He has been a community leader for 4 years, working to connect people and organizations in the tech industry and foster a spirit of collaboration and innovation.

Victor is also a product designer, He also has a background in graphic design, and he enjoys working with beautiful images and astute systems thinking. He believes that a digital product is a living thing with the ability to change and advance through time.

In addition to his work as a tech evangelist and product designer, Victor is also a musician and a community leader passionate about web3 technology. He is the founder of Great Minds in Tech Foundation, a foundation that teaches young people how to create using webflow and other design and development tools. He is also a member of the Twitter Developers community, the Polkadot community, and the Webflow Port Harcourt community.

Victor is a passionate and dedicated individual who is committed to making a difference in the world through technology. He is an inspiration to many, and he is sure to continue to make a positive impact on the tech community for many years to come.
